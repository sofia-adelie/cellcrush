Empowering patients – one world at a time

Mission

XR is transforming how clinicians train and practice — but patients still rely on pamphlets, diagrams, and long explanations to make sense of complex treatments.

For many families, especially children, biology remains abstract and frightening. Low health literacy leads to confusion, anxiety, and a sense of powerlessness.

CellCrush exists to make the invisible world inside the body visible, interactive, and empowering.

Concept

CellCrush places patients inside a stylised bloodstream where they can explore how their bodies work from the inside out. Through simple, engaging interactions, players learn about cells, systems, and biological processes in a way that reduces fear and improves understanding.

The bloodstream is just the beginning. CellCrush is built as a scalable XR universe that expands into other systems of the body, forming a patient-centered platform for immersive health education.

  • Bloodstream – current prototype
  • Immune system – future module
  • Lungs, digestive system, brain – long-term roadmap
